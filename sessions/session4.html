Session 4

04 February 2023

Notes

While in Nicodranas, Lyle, Cornelius, and Hee went to Trax's Stable's and met Irgorn. Irgorn was trying to greatly overcharge the group, which made Cornelius upset. After haggling a bit, Cornelius finally dropped that they were the group that saved Yussa. Irgorn asked Bob to verify that this was the group that saved Yussa, and Bobby verified that they did bring Yussa back. Irgorn gave them a mule and charged the group 1gp for a chicken and cage and 7sp for saddlebags. The group named the mule Bob and the chicken Irgorn. They left Nicodranas and nothing eventful happened the first day of travel except spotting an adult dragon flying over them. Both Cornelius and Lyle read 30 minutes of their books (Lyle - Draconia) the first night. Nothing exciting happened except the next morning Cornelius noticed that his and Hee's stuff had been rifled through. But nothing was taken. During their travels, Cornelius asked Hee to see his book. Cornelius determined that the book was exaggerated stories about Dragonsbane Davies but some of the information about the dragons in the book might be accurate. Hee deceived Cornelius by pretending that he could read the book and thanked Cornelius. While on the road, the Cornelius, Lyle, and Hee passed a group of cultists that had a chained man in the midst of them. The man was able to free himself from his gag and begged the group for help. The group pretended to ignore the man and passed the cultists, attacking them from behind. Blink, the captured man, was released by the lock picking of Lyle. After Blink got free he casted "Sleep" and quickly fell asleep for most of the battle. Lyle, Cornelius and Hee successfully beat the cultists.

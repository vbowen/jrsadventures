Locations

Name Description
Clovis Concord A confederation of the city-states along the Menagerie Coast and settlements in the Swavain Islands.
Has friendly relations with the Dwendalian Empire and Marquet. Neutral with everyone else, regardless of relations with the Dwendalian Empire and Marquet.
Nicodranas A major port city on the Menagerie Coast. Under the control of the Clovis Concord, except for the Open Quay district which is owned by Ahmed Noke.

Broken up into 4 different districts:
  • Restless Wharf
  • Open Quay
  • Opal Archways
  • The Skew
It contains the following points of interest:
  • Mother's Lighthouse
  • Wayfarer's Cove
  • Tidepeak
  • Lavish Chateau
  • Marquis Demesne
Demographics:
  • 68% Human
  • 13% Halfling
  • 8% Dwarves
  • 10% Other Races

Yussa's Tower Yussa brought us here following the battle with his apprentice. It was a magical building where the hallway of bedrooms seemed to magically form to our personal interests. Lyle's room was reminiscent of his home with a thatch made bed, tent like atmosphere and a fireplace that felt like something you'd create out in the woods. There was a dining room down the hall with a large table with high top chairs. Around the room were paintings that seemed to paint the history and likeness of Nicodranas.
